Our Vision

For Small Business Owners

As a small business owner, staying on top of outstanding payments is crucial for maintaining a healthy cash flow. With VroPay, we take the hassle out of payment collection. Our 'Get My MoneyBack' feature allows you to set up an e-mandate that automatically debits the outstanding credit amount from your customers on the agreed due date. This ensures you never have to chase clients for payments again.

Once the payment is processed, the funds are directly transferred into your business account, improving liquidity and saving you valuable time. No more chasing overdue invoices—let VroPay handle the financial legwork for you, so you can focus on growing your business.

  • Automated payment collection for smoother cash flow.

  • Direct transfers to your business account for faster access to funds.

  • Eliminate the risk of missed or delayed payments with a secure, automatic system.

For Working Professionals

Managing personal finances can sometimes be stressful, especially when you lend money to friends or family. The uncertainty of repayment and the awkwardness of asking for money back can strain relationships. VroPay makes it simple and transparent by providing you with the tools to create e-mandate agreements before transferring money to loved ones.

With VroPay, once an agreement is made, the repayment is automated. The agreed amount is automatically debited from the borrower’s account on the set date, ensuring that repayments happen without any awkward conversations or delays. You can finally enjoy peace of mind knowing that your personal transactions are being handled efficiently.

  • Set up e-mandates to automate repayments for personal loans.

  • No more awkward conversations about money—automated payments do the talking.

  • Rest easy knowing your funds will be returned on time and securely.

For Students

Being a student comes with many financial challenges, especially when managing money between friends or family. You may need to borrow money, or lend some to your peers. However, the confusion around repayments can create unnecessary stress. With VroPay, you can set up e-mandates to automate the repayment process, ensuring that money moves securely and on time without the need for constant reminders.

Whether you're borrowing money for textbooks or lending to a friend for a lunch, VroPay provides you with a secure, automated system that handles all repayments. This ensures that you can focus on your studies, without worrying about keeping track of payments or chasing down those who owe you money.

  • Set up automated e-mandates to simplify money transfers between you and your peers.

  • Keep track of payments without the hassle of manually following up on repayments.

  • Focus on your studies while VroPay handles your financial agreements securely and efficiently.
